# Project Experience — Overview

This domain aggregates what was learned across three active projects: `kb-for-agents` (building this knowledge base system), `mcp-telegram-bot` (a production Telegram bot via MCP), and `personal-brand-ai` (AI-assisted content creation). It also includes the operational recipes (ops notes) for maintaining the notes corpus itself.

These are project logs and maintenance recipes, not conceptual notes. They record what happened, what blocked progress, and what was resolved — the raw material for distilling decisions and patterns into other domains.
