Just finished reading "Attention Is All You Need" (https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762).

The evaluation methodology is worth borrowing, separate from the results. Several ideas here transfer directly to agent pipeline design, even though that wasn't the paper's framing. The core claim held up better than I expected when I applied it to my own setup. The pattern shows up in how RAG-vs-compiled-knowledge tradeoffs should actually be evaluated.
