Just finished reading "Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models" (https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903).

The core claim held up better than I expected when I applied it to my own setup. The failure cases section was more useful to me than the success cases. The evaluation methodology is worth borrowing, separate from the results. I can see a direct application to how I structure context for long agent sessions. I'll try the main heuristic on a real codebase next week to see if it generalises.
